# Marketing Strategy — Nonprofit & Fundraising Marketing Kit

## Market Opportunity

### Primary Market
- 1.5M+ registered 501(c)(3) organizations in the US
- $557B in charitable giving in 2023 (record high, still growing)
- 90% of nonprofits have annual budgets under $500K — no dedicated marketing staff
- Average development director manages 3-5 campaigns per year, each requiring a fresh donor appeal

### Secondary Market
- 300K+ development consultants and fundraising agencies
- 10K+ universities with annual fund and major gifts programs
- 6,000+ hospitals with foundations running annual fundraising campaigns
- 350,000+ faith communities (churches, synagogues, mosques) running annual stewardship campaigns

### Underserved Segment: Small/Mid Nonprofits
- Small nonprofits (budgets $50K-$2M) can't afford:
  - Development consultants ($150-300/hr)
  - Bloomerang ($99-499/month — database only, no copy)
  - AFP membership ($250/year) + training events
- They write donor appeals from scratch using Word templates from 2011
- **This is the exact buyer who pays $29 without blinking**
