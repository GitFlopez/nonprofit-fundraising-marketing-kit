## What This Skill Does

Generates a complete nonprofit marketing and fundraising content system — donor appeal letters, volunteer recruitment campaigns, annual report narratives, and event fundraising kits — all aligned with IRS 501(c)(3) communication standards and fundraising best practices.

Built for:
- Nonprofit executive directors and development officers
- Development consultants writing appeals for multiple organizations
- Board members tasked with fundraising communication
- Agencies managing nonprofit accounts
- Individual fundraisers at hospitals, universities, arts organizations, and faith communities

---

## The 4 Prompts

### Prompt 1 — Donor Appeal Letter Suite (FREE)
**What it generates:**
- Acquisition letter (first-time donors): problem narrative + proof + ask + P.S.
- Renewal letter (lapsed donors): relationship reactivation + impact proof + renewed ask
- Major donor upgrade letter: personalized appeal for 10x gift escalation
- Year-end giving appeal: urgency + tax deduction reminder + matching gift hook
- 5 subject lines per letter (email version)
- Segmentation guide: how to adjust each letter for 5 donor segments

**Format:** Each letter 400-600 words, direct mail + email-ready, USPS indicia note, CAN-SPAM compliant for email version.

**Inputs needed:**
- Organization name and mission (1 sentence)
- Specific program or cause being funded
- One concrete impact story (beneficiary name optional)
- Dollar amounts for 3 giving levels
- Executive director / development officer name
- Matching gift opportunity (if available)

---

### Prompt 2 — Volunteer Recruitment Campaign
**What it generates:**
- Volunteer opportunity descriptions (3 roles, 150 words each)
- Volunteer recruitment email (cold outreach to local businesses + individuals)
- Social media recruitment kit: 4 posts per platform (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn)
- Volunteer retention email: thank-you + impact update + reinvitation sequence (3 emails)
- FAQ page copy for volunteer section of website
- Corporate volunteer program pitch: 1-page proposal for employer-sponsored volunteer days

**Format:** All copy platform-ready with character counts; LinkedIn posts follow thought leadership format; corporate pitch structured as ROI case for employer (team building, CSR, tax).

**Inputs needed:**
- Organization name and mission
- 3 volunteer roles (title, time commitment, skills needed)
- Impact metric (e.g., "volunteers served 3,200 meals last year")
- Volunteer coordinator name and contact

---

### Prompt 3 — Annual Report Narrative & Impact Story System
**What it generates:**
- Annual report narrative arc (executive summary → year in review → financials story → forward look): 800-1,200 words
- 3 beneficiary impact stories (format: before → intervention → after → metric): 300 words each
- Board chair letter: credibility + momentum + ask (250 words)
- Donor recognition language: 5 giving tier descriptions (naming + benefit copy)
- Headline statistics panel: 8 impact metrics formatted for infographic callouts
- Planned giving mention: 1 paragraph introducing legacy giving program

**Format:** Narrative sections ready for desktop publishing; statistics formatted as [NUMBER] [LABEL] pairs; impact stories follow CARS framework (Context, Action, Result, So What).

**Inputs needed:**
- Organization name, fiscal year, mission
- 3 program areas and their key metrics
- Total budget and % to programs (vs. admin/fundraising)
- 1 beneficiary story (raw details OK — prompt polishes)
- Board chair name and tenure
- Year-over-year donation growth (or decline, with explanation)

---

### Prompt 4 — Fundraising Event Marketing Kit
**What it generates:**
- Event concept brief: theme, target audience, venue brief, sponsorship tiers
- Save-the-date + invitation copy (formal mail + email versions)
- Sponsorship prospectus: 4 tiers (Presenting $10K / Gold $5K / Silver $2.5K / Friend $1K) with benefits matrix
- 6-email promotional sequence: announcement → countdown → speaker/honoree feature → last chance → day-of → follow-up thank-you with giving link
- Auctioneer / emcee script: welcome (3 min), mission moment (2 min), fund-a-need appeal (5 min, 3 emotional beats)
- Post-event donor conversion email: attendee → annual fund donor pathway
- Social media event recap kit: 5 posts + 1 Instagram Story sequence

**Format:** Sponsorship prospectus formatted for print/PDF; emcee script timed with speaker notes; email sequence ready for MailChimp/Constant Contact/Salesforce Nonprofit; event recap social posts include caption + image brief.

**Inputs needed:**
- Organization name and mission
- Event name, date, venue, ticket price
- Honoree or featured speaker (optional)
- Beneficiary program being funded (1 sentence)
- Executive director / development director name
- Sponsorship deadline and event capacity
