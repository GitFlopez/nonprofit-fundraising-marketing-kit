# Nonprofit & Fundraising Marketing Kit

**Generate donor appeals, volunteer campaigns, annual report narratives, and event kits — with IRS 501(c)(3) compliance built in.**

---

## The Problem

Nonprofits run lean. The development director is also the program director, the grant writer, and the person setting up chairs for the gala. They don't have time to write four donor appeal letters, a volunteer recruitment campaign, and an annual report narrative every quarter — and they can't afford to hire the consultant who charges $300/hour to do it.

Generic AI tools generate marketing copy that sounds like it was written for a SaaS startup. It violates IRS tax deduction language rules. It ignores CAN-SPAM requirements for email fundraising. It has no fund-a-need appeal structure, no donor segmentation logic, and no idea what a CARS framework impact story is.

This skill fixes all of that.

## Example Output

Built for: **Desert Hope Community Resource Center** (Henderson, NV)
- Mission: Food security + workforce development for families in poverty
- Program funded: Summer meals program (1,200 children, 12 weeks)

**Sample from Prompt 1 — Renewal Letter:**
> "Last summer, Marcus Torres almost didn't make it to our summer meals program. His single mom, working double shifts at the casino, couldn't get him to our site before her shift started. Then our volunteer coordinator, Rosa, arranged a pickup route.
>
> Marcus ate 72 meals that summer. In September, he started 6th grade two inches taller and — his teacher told us — more focused than she'd ever seen him.
>
> That's what your $50 gift did last year. Will you renew today and feed another Marcus this summer?"

**Sample from Prompt 4 — Fund-a-Need Appeal Script:**
> "I want to ask you to do something right now. Take out your phone. [PAUSE] In front of you, I'm going to tell you what $500 does. Not in theory. Not in a brochure. Here, tonight.
>
> $500 feeds one child for the entire summer. [PAUSE] Twelve weeks. Sixty meals. [PAUSE] One child who will come back to school in August and actually be able to learn.
>
> I'm going to ask the people in this room to feed 50 children tonight. Will you be one of them?"

## Why Not Just Use ChatGPT?

ChatGPT will write you a donor letter. It will also forget to include IRS-required language about no goods or services being exchanged. It will write a fund-a-need appeal with no emotional arc, no pause structure, and no ask. It will generate a sponsorship prospectus with generic tier names and no benefits matrix.

This skill was built by studying how Bloomerang, AFP (Association of Fundraising Professionals), and major development consultants teach appeal writing — then encoding those frameworks directly into the prompts so you don't have to.
